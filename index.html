About Us


Pizzas are shipped Monday through Friday using FedEx. Because we want you to receive your pizza frozen, We cannot have packages in transit over the weekend, so we are not able to delivery your package on a Sunday, Monday, or on a FedEx Holiday.

HOW DO WE DO IT?

We pack your pizza in a cooler within a shipping box with enough dry ice to keep the contents frozen well into the evening of the delivery date. It is best to open the package immediately upon arrival and to put your pizza in the freezer. Use gloves when handling dry ice. Fun Fact: the temperature inside that box is about negative 110 degrees F which is colder than your freezer by 100 degrees.

IN A RUSH? UPGRADE!

All expedited orders received by Noon CST Monday through Friday will be processed same day. Overnight orders for Saturday delivery must be received by noon or they will be shipped the following Monday. 2 day express orders received on Thursday or Friday will be shipped the following Monday. Any order received after noon CST may not be shipped same day. We encourage you to read our shipping policies or call us if you have questions.

OUR GUARANTEE

To guarantee delivery we must have the complete and correct address. We are not able to deliver to PO Boxes, A.P.O or F.P.O addresses due the highly perishable nature of frozen pizza.

RECEIPTS

All orders include a packing slip with no pricing information. We are unable to ship outside of the United States but an order may be placed internationally for delivery within the US by using PayPal.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

We are here to help you. Please chat with our customer service team during business hours of 9:00 AM until 4:00 PM (CST) and if we are not online please send us an email and we will respond as quickly as possible.
